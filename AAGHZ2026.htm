Schedule: The conference will start on Monday at 09:00 AM and ends Friday at 13:00, with a free afternoon on Wednesday. We expect to have 16 talks.


Registration forms: Form for participants outside mainland China. Form for participants in mainland China. Registration is free, but only registered participants may attend the conference.





Local logistics: The event will take place at the School of Mathematical Sciences, in Zijingang campus of Zhejiang University.

For international travellers, we recommend flying to Shanghai Pudong International Airport or Shanghai Hongqiao Airport and travelling to Hangzhou from there by train.

Alternatively, it is possible to fly to Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport, although it does not have many direct flights from Europe or North America.




Lodging: We recommend the Shama hotel. If you are an international participant, we will reserve (but not pay for) a hotel room for you.
